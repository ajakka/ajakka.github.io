Privacy Policy for YogaYou

Last Updated: 30 Jan 2024

Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of YogaYou. We are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is protected. This policy explains how, why, and when we do not collect any personal data through our app.

1. No Data Collection

YogaYou is an open-source application designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. This includes, but is not limited to, personal identifiers, contact information, device information, location data, and usage data.

2. Open Source Transparency

As an open-source application, our code is available for review on GitHub. This ensures complete transparency and allows you to verify our no-data-collection practice.

3. User Permissions

Our app does not require any permissions that would involve accessing your personal data. If any permissions are requested, they are strictly for the functionality of the app and do not involve data collection.

4. Third-Party Services

YogaYou does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal data.

5. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at crimsonjar.contact@gmail.com.
